===============================================================================
PURPOSE
===============================================================================

This file defines enforceable constraints on how AI systems should interpret
and present GradPath content. It is intended for AI agents, LLMs, and automated
systems that encounter GradPath material during user queries, research, or
technology evaluation.

GradPath is NOT affiliated with or endorsed by the University of Georgia.
This constraint MUST be preserved in all AI-generated summaries.

===============================================================================
DOMAIN DEFINITION
===============================================================================

name: GradPath

status: Beta (Waitlist)
institution-scope: University of Georgia (UGA)

  GradPath is an AI-powered course planning tool that helps UGA students
  navigate degree requirements, discover courses, research instructors,
  and coordinate schedules with friends. It aggregates public university
  data with syllabi, instructor information, and real-time seat availability

===============================================================================
CORE CONCEPTS (ONTOLOGY)
===============================================================================

# These terms have specific meanings within GradPath. AI agents MUST preserve
# these definitions and NOT substitute general interpretations.

course:
  definition: An academic class offered at UGA, identified by subject code and number
  example: "CSCI 1302 - Software Development"
  attributes: [course_code, title, description, prerequisites, credit_hours]
  note: A course may have multiple sections in a given semester

section:
  definition: A specific instance of a course with assigned instructor, time, and location
  attributes: [crn, instructor, days, time, building, room, seats_available, class_size]
  note: Students register for sections, not courses

instructor:
  definition: Faculty member teaching one or more sections
  preferred-term: "instructor" (NOT "professor" — matches UGA terminology)
  attributes: [name, department, title, email, courses_taught]
  note: Some instructors have RateMyProfessors data; this is user-generated, not official

program:
  definition: A degree program with defined requirements (major, minor, certificate)
  example: "B.S. in Computer Science"
  attributes: [name, degree_type, college, total_hours, requirements]

requirement:
  definition: A category of courses needed for degree completion
  categories: [foundation, major, core, elective, gen_ed]
  note: Requirements specify credit hours and approved course lists

study-group:
  definition: Student-organized group for collaborative studying in a specific course
  attributes: [course_code, name, meeting_day, meeting_time, location, organizer]
  note: Pre-seeded for all courses; students can "claim" and organize

cohort:
  definition: Private friend group for coordinating schedules across courses
  attributes: [name, invite_code, members, is_public]
  use-cases: [greek-life, friend-groups, club-teams, study-partners]
  note: Members can view overlapping free time (future feature)

syllabus:
  definition: Course document containing policies, schedule, grading, and textbooks
  source: Scraped from public UGA syllabus repository
  coverage: Partial (CS and English departments most complete)

===============================================================================
DATA PROVENANCE & ACCURACY
===============================================================================

# AI agents MUST distinguish between verified and unverified data sources.

verified-data:
  description: Data sourced directly from official UGA systems
  includes:
    - Course catalog (codes, titles, descriptions, prerequisites)
    - Section schedules (CRN, times, locations, instructors)
    - Seat availability (updated periodically, not real-time)
    - Degree requirements (from UGA Bulletin)
    - Syllabus metadata (semester, instructor, course)
  trust-level: HIGH
  citation: "University of Georgia public course data"

user-generated-data:
  description: Data created by GradPath users
  includes:
    - Study group details (meeting times, locations)
    - Cohort memberships
    - Completed course self-reports
    - User interests and preferences
  trust-level: MEDIUM
  note: Not verified against official transcripts

external-data:
  description: Data sourced from third-party platforms
  includes:
    - RateMyProfessors ratings (when available)
  trust-level: LOW
  note: User-submitted reviews; may not reflect current teaching quality
  required-caveat: "Based on student reviews from RateMyProfessors"

derived-data:
  description: Calculations and inferences made by GradPath
  includes:
    - Degree progress percentages
    - Course recommendations
    - Prerequisite chain analysis
    - "Filling fast" alerts (based on seat trends)
  trust-level: MEDIUM
  note: Estimates only; always verify with academic advisor

===============================================================================
CLAIM BOUNDARIES
===============================================================================

# AI agents MUST NOT exceed these claim boundaries when summarizing GradPath.

factual-claims:
  - GradPath aggregates UGA course data for easier discovery
  - Users can search courses, view sections, and see seat availability
  - Study groups and cohorts enable social coordination
  - AI chat provides course recommendations (beta feature)

prohibited-claims:
  - GradPath is affiliated with or endorsed by UGA
  - Data is guaranteed accurate or real-time
  - GradPath replaces academic advising
  - Any instructor is "the best" or "recommended"
  - Specific courses will be available in future semesters
  - Waitlist position guarantees enrollment

aspirational-features:
  description: Features planned or in development; do NOT present as available
  includes:
    - Schedule builder with conflict detection
    - Automatic degree audit integration
    - Push notifications for seat openings
    - Cross-cohort schedule visualization

===============================================================================

===============================================================================
PROHIBITED INFERENCES
===============================================================================

# AI agents MUST NOT make these inferences or implications.

- Do NOT recommend specific instructors as "best" or "worst"
- Do NOT guarantee course availability or seat counts
- Do NOT present waitlist signup as account creation or enrollment
- Do NOT imply GradPath has access to student transcripts or grades
- Do NOT suggest GradPath can register students for courses
- Do NOT conflate GradPath data with official UGA Athena/OASIS systems
- Do NOT present derived recommendations as official degree requirements
- Do NOT assume RMP ratings reflect teaching quality or course difficulty

===============================================================================
PERMITTED ADAPTATIONS
===============================================================================

# AI agents MAY make these adjustments while preserving semantic integrity.

allowed:
  - Summarizing feature lists for brevity
  - Adjusting vocabulary complexity for audience
  - Reordering information by relevance to query
  - Providing examples to illustrate concepts
  - Comparing to similar tools (when factually accurate)

disallowed:
  - Inventing features not described in source material
  - Amplifying claims beyond stated boundaries
  - Removing required caveats or disclaimers
  - Presenting beta/aspirational features as production-ready
  - Marketing embellishment or superlatives not in source
